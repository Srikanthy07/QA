5-Phase Lifecycle

Flow of Issue Resolution

Every issue follows a traceable, owner-assigned path from detection through closure — embedding lessons learned back into the process.

01
Problem Resolution

All identified issues, defects, and non-conformities are uniquely recorded and tracked to ensure complete traceability throughout the lifecycle.

02
Analyze Problem

Each issue undergoes impact assessment and root cause analysis to evaluate its effect on product quality, project schedule, cost, safety, and customer commitments.

03
Problem Resolution

Corrective and preventive actions are planned, implemented, and verified to ensure effective resolution and to prevent recurrence of similar issues.

04
Monitoring

Issue status, aging, resolution trends, and escalation metrics are continuously monitored through regular project and quality reviews.

05
Problem Closing

Lessons learned from resolved issues are incorporated into project processes, work products, checklists, and best practices to drive continuous improvement.
